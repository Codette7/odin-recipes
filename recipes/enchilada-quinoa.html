Enchilada Quinoa

This enchilada dish comes together quickly and freezes well for a delicious protein-packed side dish.

This recipe couldn't be easier - just add the ingredients and heat for long enough to cook the quinoa. And can't forget to top with cheese. Voila!

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can yellow corn
  • 2 cans red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can diced fire roasted tomatoes
  • 4oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican style cheese

Steps

  1. Combine all canned ingredients and water in a large pot and bring to a simmer.
  2. Add quinoa and simmer for 15 minutes or until quinoa has softened.
  3. Stir in cream cheese until fully dissolved.
  4. Top with shredded cheese and serve.
